F. A. Q.

How do I play the game?
Use the arrow keys to move your spaceship and the space button to shoot. Destroy asteroids before they hit you, and avoid collisions to survive as long as possible.
What is the goal of the game?
The objective is to score as many points as possible by blasting asteroids until they disappear. The longer you survive, the higher your score.
Is there a way to save my score?
Your highest score is automatically saved on your device. You can check it in the "High Scores" section.
Is multiplayer available?
Currently, the game is single‑player only. We’re exploring multiplayer options for future updates.
